Company Overview

You can't defragment
what you can't measure

The average dental practice runs on 20+ disconnected tools. None share a payment processor. None share a patient record. Every tool is an island — and dentists are paying for the chaos. 70% of practices are currently vulnerable to financial fraud and embezzlement as a result.

Prahsys built the financial layer first. Money touches every part of a dental practice. By owning the financial ledger — the single source of truth for every dollar — every layer built on top has context no standalone tool can match.

The strategy is modelled on Toast in restaurants: give solutions that grow customer revenue, and more revenue means more payments processed through Prahsys. Each new layer deepens the relationship and widens the moat.

The Problem
20+ software tools per practice. 6 separate merchant accounts minimum. 70% fraud vulnerability. 0 dominant fintech players in dental. The market is wide open.
The Foundation
Prahsys owns the financial ledger. One unified merchant account, one source of truth for every dollar. Gateway + Processor + Payops collapsed into a single API-native integration.
The Moat
Three layers — Payments, Patients, PrognosiX — that no competitor can replicate without owning all three. Each practice adds data that makes every layer smarter.
Why Now
Payments infrastructure is live. Agentic AI makes the patient layer possible at a cost that wasn't viable three years ago. No dominant player exists. Network effects are starting.

Prahsys Payments
The financial foundation. A single merchant account replacing 6+ for every payment channel a dental practice needs.
  • Unified merchant account — card present, CNP, text-to-pay
  • Single financial ledger — every dollar in one place
  • API-native — gateway + processor + payops collapsed
  • Real-time fraud and embezzlement detection
  • 106 basis points net per dollar processed
  • $0 hidden fees. 5 min setup. Free terminal.
Initial Release
Prahsys Patients
Agentic AI built on top of the financial ledger. Because Prahsys owns the financial data, these AI agents know more than any standalone patient tool ever could.
  • AI fills cancelled slots from ranked waitlists automatically
  • Personalised patient recall based on payment and clinical history
  • In-house membership plans built into the financial layer
  • Plain-language AI tasks — "send recall to every patient 6 months overdue"
  • Unified inbox — SMS, email, portal all tied to patient context
  • Lab cases and referral workflow automation
FDA 510(k) Submitted
PrognosiX AI
Clinical AI that closes the loop. A diagnosis in PrognosiX triggers a treatment plan, which connects to a payment plan, which triggers automated patient follow-up. No manual handoff.
  • First true 3D X-ray AI to market
  • Patent pending — trained on proprietary Prahsys data
  • Pixel-level CBCT imaging analysis with colour mapping
  • Connects clinical finding directly to financial outcome
  • $399/month per practice at launch
  • ~70% EBITDA margins on AI revenue

PrognosiX is an investigational device under development and is not approved for clinical diagnosis or treatment decisions. Subject to FDA 510(k) clearance.
